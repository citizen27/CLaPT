Panther logs are useful to determine issues with rollbacks, upgrade or system failures. Particularly useful after updates on Windows systems. 

Collect if needed, pretty low importance in most use cases.

Panther logs get really granular and annoying. Better artifacts exist, and see the link below for how convoluted these get. 

https://support.microsoft.com/en-gb/topic/log-files-that-are-created-when-you-upgrade-to-a-new-version-of-windows-9ec8aa31-0cc1-a0b2-2d98-e9c6714349b9
